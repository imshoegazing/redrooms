Red Rooms

Design Hotel

Hotel

This first-class hotel lies in Berlin's Friedrichshain district, close to Ostkreuz and Warschauer Straße. The modern, stylish Red Rooms include a laptop safe and a TV with satellite channels. A hairdryer and bathrobes are provided in the bathroom. Guests receive a free bottle of mineral water on arrival. Red Rooms rooftop has a large sunbathing area. The terrace offers great views of Berlin. Red Rooms Hotel is a 5-minute walk from the tram stop at Warschauer Straße. Numerous amount of bars, restaurants and fancy shops are all around. Wi-Fi is free for all our guests.
